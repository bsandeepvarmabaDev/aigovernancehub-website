Factual information for vendor review, security questionnaires, and purchase approval. We document only capabilities that exist today.

Pricing approach

Self-service payment

Razorpay Secure Checkout for website assessments. Order total is computed server-side; amount is verified before report release. We do not store card or UPI data.

Enterprise invoice & PO

Enterprise quotes may include a secure payment link or recorded payment after sales review. For procurement coordination, invoice arrangements, and PO references, contact sales@aigovernancehub.ai.

Deliverables

Support SLAs

General support: 1 business day. Enterprise sales: 1–2 business days. Security: security@aigovernancehub.ai.

Refunds

Questions procurement teams ask sales

If your answer is not below, email sales@aigovernancehub.ai.

How do I purchase for my organization?

Self-serve: corporate email at checkout on this website. Enterprise: submit via the assessment wizard (1,001+ work items) or email sales@ with portfolio size and procurement requirements. You receive a Request ID for tracking.

How do I get an invoice after self-serve checkout?

Your Razorpay payment confirmation email serves as your receipt. For a formal GST invoice, email support@aigovernancehub.ai with your payment reference and billing details.

How do we upgrade to continuous Jira governance?

After your Executive Assessment, install the Marketplace app for ongoing reviews and approvals inside Jira. Website assessments and Marketplace subscriptions are separate purchases.

Can we pay by purchase order or invoice?

Enterprise assessments (typically 1,001+ work items or procurement requirements) are coordinated through sales. PO references and invoice arrangements are handled case-by-case — contact sales with your procurement requirements.

Do you support USD / EUR pricing?

Self-serve checkout displays INR via Razorpay with currency selection where supported. Enterprise quotes can be scoped in your preferred currency through sales.

What is the vendor legal entity and tax documentation?

Provide your procurement template to sales@aigovernancehub.ai. We respond within 1–2 business days with available registration and tax details.

Is there a SOC 2 or ISO 27001 report?

We publish our Security Policy and Trust Center documentation. We do not claim SOC 2 or ISO 27001 certification unless separately contracted and documented. Security questionnaires: security@aigovernancehub.ai.

Where is assessment data processed and stored?

Website uploads are processed on our serverless infrastructure with TLS in transit and secure persistent storage for report delivery. Production Jira workflows run in your Atlassian tenant via Forge. See Trust Center for retention (90-day reports, ~24-hour pending upload sessions).

Can we get a DPA or BAA?

Contact security@aigovernancehub.ai with your requirements. We do not represent that the website assessment alone satisfies HIPAA or banking regulatory obligations.

What deliverables are included in the contract?

Executive AI Governance Assessment in HTML, PDF, Word, and PowerPoint; dashboard access; 90-day recovery unless deletion requested. Exact scope confirmed in enterprise quote.

What is the refund process?

See Refund Policy. Refund status may affect download access per policy terms.

How does this differ from the Atlassian Marketplace app?

Website → Executive Governance Assessment (export upload, Razorpay, one-time deliverables). Marketplace → Continuous AI Governance inside Jira (production workflows, Atlassian billing). Separate products and purchase flows.

Can we run a pilot before enterprise rollout?

Yes — self-serve assessment from ₹199 with free preview, or Marketplace Try it free for Jira teams. Enterprise pilots above 1,000 work items are scoped via sales.
