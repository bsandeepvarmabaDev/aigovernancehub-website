NIST AI RMF Governance Workflows for Jira Cloud

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework helps organizations structure AI risk management around Govern, Map, Measure and Manage. AI Governance Hub helps teams operationalize these activities through Jira-based workflows.

Purpose: This page explains how AI Governance Hub can support governance readiness activities. It is not legal advice. Organizations should validate regulatory obligations with qualified legal, compliance and risk teams.

Govern

Define ownership, review accountability, governance workflows and approval responsibilities for AI initiatives.

Map

Capture AI use case context, stakeholders, intended purpose, vendors and operating environment.

Measure

Assess risk indicators including data sensitivity, customer impact, automation, third-party dependencies and compliance exposure.

Manage

Track review actions, approvals, remediation notes, evidence and reporting for ongoing governance oversight.

How AI Governance Hub connects framework requirements to Jira workflows

  • Register AI use cases, owners, vendors and business context.
  • Assess risk across privacy, security, customer impact, automation and regulated-domain factors.
  • Route reviews to security, privacy, responsible AI, legal and business stakeholders.
  • Maintain approval history, evidence references, audit notes and governance decisions.
  • Provide executive-ready summaries for AI governance, compliance and risk stakeholders.
