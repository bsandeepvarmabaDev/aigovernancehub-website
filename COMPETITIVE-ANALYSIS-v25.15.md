# Competitive Analysis — v25.15

## Positioning statement (v25.15)

**AI Governance Hub** = operational AI governance **assessment** from Jira/Azure/CSV exports, with server-validated scoring and payment-gated executive reports — plus a **separate** Jira Marketplace app for ongoing governance workflows.

## vs. Marketplace governance apps

| Dimension | Marketplace apps | AI Governance Hub |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|
| Entry | Jira install + subscription | Website assessment from ₹199 |
| Output | In-Jira workflows | Board-ready HTML/PDF/DOCX/PPTX |
| Best for | Production Jira teams | Evaluation, PMO, pre-rollout |
| **Weakness** | Requires Jira commitment | Website must work — **prod broken** |

## vs. Static report generators

| Dimension | Static tools | AI Governance Hub |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Validation | Often client-side | Server-side |
| Payment | Varies | Razorpay verify before release |
| Security scan | Rare | CSV/XSS scan |
| **Strength** | Simple | Enterprise gates + audit |

## vs. AI governance consulting

| Dimension | Consulting | AI Governance Hub |
|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| Time | Weeks | Minutes |
| Cost | ₹ lakhs+ | From ₹199 |
| Repeatability | Low | High |
| **Weakness** | Depth of bespoke advice | Not a substitute for legal sign-off |

1. **Fast, defensible AI portfolio snapshot** from existing Jira exports  
2. **Executive formats** procurement and boards expect  
3. **Path to production** via Marketplace app  

## Weakest points

1. **Production deploy failure** — fatal for any competitor comparison  
2. No public customer logos  
3. DNS/email hardening incomplete
