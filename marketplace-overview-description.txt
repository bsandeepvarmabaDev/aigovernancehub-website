AI Governance Hub is an enterprise AI governance platform for Jira Cloud that helps organizations manage AI reviews, risk assessments, approvals, vendor governance, evidence collection, compliance readiness, and audit reporting within a centralized workflow.

As organizations adopt AI technologies across business functions, governance activities often become fragmented across spreadsheets, emails, documents, and disconnected review processes. AI Governance Hub provides a structured governance framework that enables teams to evaluate, review, approve, monitor, and report on AI initiatives using Jira Cloud.

Key capabilities include:

• AI Use Case Registration and Inventory Management
• AI Risk and Impact Assessments
• Security, Privacy, Legal, Responsible AI and Business Reviews
• Governance Approval Workflows
• Vendor Governance and Third-Party AI Assessments
• Evidence Repository and Audit Trail Management
• Compliance Readiness Reporting
• Executive Governance Dashboards and Reporting
• Governance Documentation and Review Tracking

Built on Atlassian Forge, AI Governance Hub leverages Jira Cloud to provide a secure and scalable governance experience while helping organizations improve transparency, accountability, and AI risk management practices.

The platform is designed for governance teams, risk managers, compliance teams, security teams, privacy teams, audit functions, and business stakeholders responsible for managing AI initiatives throughout their lifecycle.
