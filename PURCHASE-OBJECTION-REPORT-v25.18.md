## Final Question: vs $250,000 Consulting

**Top reasons enterprise still chooses AI Governance Hub:**

1. **Speed** — minutes to preview vs weeks of discovery workshops  
2. **Evidence** — every work item in export analyzed, not interview sampling  
3. **Cost** — pilot from ₹199 vs six-figure SOW for assessment phase alone  
4. **Repeatability** — re-run when portfolio changes without re-scoping consultants  
5. **Deliverable formats** — PDF/DOCX/PPTX ready for board same day  
6. **Operational path** — assessment → Marketplace app for ongoing Jira governance  
7. **No third-party AI training** on confidential exports  
8. **Server-verified payment** before release — commercial integrity  

**Weakness if messaging alone:** Without deploy + one permitted reference, consulting incumbency still wins board narrative.
