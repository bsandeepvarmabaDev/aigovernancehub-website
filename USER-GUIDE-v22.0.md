5. **Business Impact** — Planning estimates with documented assumptions (not financial guarantees)
6. **Department Analysis** — Scores per project/department with strengths and gaps

8. **Risk Heatmap** — Critical through Low with recommendations

10. **AI Governance Maturity** — Initial through Optimized with rationale
11. **Executive Insights** — Contextual observations from your data
12. **Recommendations** — Prioritized actions with business benefit

## Understanding Scores

**Governance Score (0–100)** — Composite of five 20-point dimensions derived from your export:
- Owner assignment on AI candidates
- Description/documentation depth
- High-risk item concentration
- Labels and priority taxonomy
- Workflow closure signals

**AI Readiness** — Combines governance score with AI candidate coverage and risk posture.

**Maturity Level** — Initial → Developing → Defined → Managed → Optimized based on composite signals.

Every score includes an explanation of **why** — not just a number.

---

## Business Impact Estimates

Financial and time-saving figures are **planning ranges** based on:
- Count of AI/automation-eligible work items
- Documented hourly rate assumption ($75/hr default for modeling)
- Industry-standard planning ranges for repetitive work

These are **not guarantees**. Replace assumptions with your organization's actual rates for internal business cases.

## Methodology

- All analysis is **server-side** from your uploaded CSV/Excel export
- **No generative AI** is used to invent content
- AI candidates detected via keyword and context matching on summary, description, labels
- Department analysis uses Project field when no explicit Department column exists
