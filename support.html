Operational troubleshooting

Self-service recovery first. Include your checkout email and payment reference when contacting support.

Payment failed or not verified

Checkout may have been interrupted. If funds were deducted, go to Recover My Report with your checkout email. Otherwise return to assessment wizard and complete Secure Checkout. Email support@aigovernancehub.ai with Razorpay payment ID.

Upload failed or validation error

Check required columns in Sample Files. Re-export CSV with all fields. Sessions expire in ~24 hours — re-upload if checkout was not completed.

Report not ready or download failed

Large portfolios may take a few minutes after payment. Use My Reports or Recover My Report. Downloads remain available for 90 days.

Email not received

Check spam. Delivery is optional — your report is available via success page downloads, My Reports, and Recover My Report. Request resend via support with order reference.

Browser closed before download

Payment is tied to your checkout email. Use Recover My Report or My Reports from any device within 90 days.

Duplicate purchase or double charge

Contact support@aigovernancehub.ai immediately with both payment references. See Refund Policy.

Refund request

Email support@ with order reference per Refund Policy. Refund may disable download access per policy terms.

Enterprise quote or invoice

Portfolios over 1,000 work items: submit via assessment wizard or email sales@aigovernancehub.ai. See Enterprise Procurement.

Recovery expired (after 90 days)

Reports are retained for 90 days unless deletion requested. Contact support@ — we cannot guarantee recovery after expiry.

How to Report a Bug

Email support@aigovernancehub.ai with your Jira site URL, app version, screenshots, steps to reproduce, expected behavior, and actual behavior.
