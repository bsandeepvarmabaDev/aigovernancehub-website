Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Governance Hub?

An Enterprise AI Governance Assessment Platform. Upload a project export, receive an Executive Assessment with governance scores, AI-related work items, and framework mapping — no Jira expertise required.

What files are supported?

Jira Cloud CSV, Azure DevOps CSV, Excel (exported/saved as UTF-8 CSV), and UTF-8 CSV exports with required columns.

How do I export from Jira?

Project → Search issues → Export → CSV (All fields). Step-by-step instructions appear in the assessment wizard.

How do I export from Azure DevOps?

Boards → Queries → Run query → Export to CSV. Use our sample Azure DevOps template as reference.

Do I need to modify my export?

No, if required columns are present. Use our templates if columns are missing.

What are the required fields?

Issue Key, Summary, Description, Issue Type, Status, and Project — each explained in the wizard with why they matter.

Can I upload custom fields?

Yes. Custom fields are optional and enrich analysis depth.

What is the compatibility check?

A pre-analysis validation showing compatibility score, detected platform, work items, projects, fields, and estimated analysis time.

How are AI-related work items identified?

We scan summaries, descriptions, and labels for AI, LLM, automation, and related governance signals.

How is the Governance Score calculated?

Based on high-risk ratio, AI-related work item coverage, and medium-risk volume across your uploaded work items.

What appears in the free preview?

Governance score, AI-related work item count, risk summary, framework coverage, executive summary, and top opportunities.

What is locked before payment?

Recommendations, heatmaps, detailed analysis, executive PDF, and department reports — premium deliverables requiring full processing.

What happens after payment?

Server-side verification → report generation → immediate download → email delivery → dashboard recovery access.

What plans are available?

Self-service: Starter (up to 50 items), Professional (up to 500), Business (up to 1,000). Enterprise Assessment for portfolios over 1,000 work items with dedicated processing and custom quote.

How does plan detection work?

We automatically recommend the minimum plan that fits your upload. We never recommend a lower plan than required.

Can I pay less than the recommended plan?

No. Your upload determines the required plan. Splitting exports may qualify for a lower tier.

How does Enterprise assessment work?

Uploads over 1,000 work items automatically receive an Enterprise Assessment. Submit your details in the wizard — our sales team responds within 1–2 business days with scoping, portfolio analysis, and a secure payment link. Email sales@aigovernancehub.ai with your Request ID.

Is payment secure?

Yes. Razorpay processes payments with server-side HMAC verification. We never store card or UPI details.

What currencies are supported?

INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, and SGD. Pricing is calculated server-side based on your selected currency.

Are taxes included?

GST (18%) is shown in your order summary before payment, calculated server-side.

Does the order summary match Razorpay?

Yes. The total payable displayed must exactly match the Razorpay checkout amount.

How long are files stored?

Upload sessions expire within 24 hours if checkout is not completed. Generated reports remain recoverable for 90 days via signed recovery tokens. Contact support for earlier deletion.

Can I recover my report?

Yes — via Recover My Report or My Reports.

What frameworks are mapped?

EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF context — supporting evidence workflows, not certification.

Is this legal compliance certification?

No. Reports support governance review and readiness evidence.

What is your refund policy?

See Refund Policy. Digital goods delivered after verified payment; case-by-case within 7 days for platform errors.

Do you support international payments?

Yes, where Razorpay international payments are available for your currency.

Who should use AI Governance Hub?

PMs, Scrum Masters, PMOs, Delivery Managers, AI Governance Teams, Enterprise Architects, CIOs, and CTOs.

How is my data secured?

SSL encryption, secure upload validation, security scanning, persistent encrypted storage, signed downloads. See Trust Center.

How do I contact support?

support@aigovernancehub.ai · sales@aigovernancehub.ai for enterprise.
