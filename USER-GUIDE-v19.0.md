# User Guide — v19.0 Enterprise Guided Assessment

**Product:** AI Governance Hub — Guided AI Governance Assessment

## Overview

The assessment wizard on [Pricing](https://aigovernancehub.ai/pricing.html) guides you from project export to full governance report — no guesswork about file format or required fields.

## Step-by-step

### 1. Choose your source

Select where your work items come from:

- **Jira Cloud** — CSV export from issue search
- **Azure DevOps** — Query CSV export
- **Excel** — .xlsx or CSV using our template
- **CSV** — UTF-8 file with required columns

GitHub, ServiceNow, Monday.com, Asana, and ClickUp are coming soon.

### 2. Export your data

Follow the on-screen export instructions for your source. Download sample files if unsure:

- Sample Jira CSV
- Sample Azure DevOps CSV
- Excel/CSV template
- Sample governance report (preview of output)

**Required columns:** Issue Key, Summary, Description, Issue Type, Status, Project — each explained in the wizard.

### 3. Upload and validate

Upload your file. We automatically check:

- File type and encoding
- Required columns present
- No duplicate columns
- Security scan
- Work item count

You'll see a **compatibility score** and whether you're ready for assessment.

### 4. Plan detection

| Plan | Work items | Self-serve |
|------|------------|------------|
| Starter | Up to 50 | Yes — ₹199 via Razorpay |
| Professional | 51–500 | Contact sales |
| Business | 501–5,000 | Contact sales |
| Enterprise | 5,000+ or 5+ projects | Contact sales |

Large uploads show **Enterprise Assessment Detected** with sales contact options — no self-serve checkout.

### 5. Preview (free)

See AI candidates, governance score, risk summary, executive summary, framework mapping, and top opportunities before paying.

Locked until payment: detailed recommendations, full inventory, PDF, department analysis, heatmaps.

### 6. Unlock your assessment

Enter your name and email, then click **Unlock Your Assessment**.

You receive:

- Executive PDF-ready report
- Governance score breakdown
- AI candidate report
- Risk matrix
- Recommendations
- Framework mapping
- Email copy + recovery access

Payment is secured by Razorpay. AI Governance Hub never stores card data.

## After payment

Download immediately on the success page. Recover anytime via [My Reports](https://aigovernancehub.ai/login.html) or [Recover My Report](https://aigovernancehub.ai/recover-report.html).
