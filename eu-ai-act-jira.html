EU AI Act Compliance Workflows for Jira Cloud

Updated: June 2026

AI Governance Hub helps organizations structure AI intake, risk review, approvals, and evidence tracking for EU AI Act readiness using Jira-native governance workflows.

1. Why EU AI Act readiness matters

The EU AI Act creates a structured regulatory model for AI systems, including obligations around risk classification, transparency, accountability, documentation, and oversight. Enterprise teams need a repeatable way to identify AI use cases, assess risk, capture decisions, and maintain evidence.

2. How AI Governance Hub helps

AI Governance Hub is designed to help Jira Cloud teams operationalize AI governance workflows instead of managing reviews across spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected documents.

  • Register AI systems, use cases, owners, and vendors.
  • Assess privacy, security, customer impact, automation, and regulated-domain risk.
  • Route reviews to security, privacy, responsible AI, legal, and business owners.
  • Capture approval decisions, comments, audit evidence, and review history.
  • Generate governance summaries for leadership and audit readiness.

3. Suggested EU AI Act readiness workflow

  • Create AI system inventory.
  • Classify AI use case and business impact.
  • Assess data sensitivity and automated decisioning risk.
  • Identify required review chain.
  • Capture evidence and approval history.
  • Track remediation actions and reporting status.

4. Best fit teams

  • AI governance teams
  • Compliance and risk teams
  • Security and privacy teams
  • Product and engineering teams
  • Jira Cloud administrators
