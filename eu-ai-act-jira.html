EU AI Act readiness

EU AI Act Compliance Workflows for Jira Cloud

The EU AI Act introduces governance expectations for organizations that develop, deploy, procure or manage AI systems. AI Governance Hub helps teams translate AI governance activity into Jira-native reviews, risk assessments, approvals, evidence and reporting.

Purpose: This page explains how AI Governance Hub can support governance readiness activities. It is not legal advice. Organizations should validate regulatory obligations with qualified legal, compliance and risk teams.

AI System Inventory

Maintain a practical inventory of AI use cases, owners, vendors, business purpose and lifecycle status.

Risk Classification Support

Capture risk indicators such as personal data, customer-facing use, automated decision support, vendor dependency and regulated-domain exposure.

Review and Approval Routing

Route AI initiatives through security, privacy, responsible AI, legal and business review paths.

Audit Evidence Tracking

Maintain evidence references, decisions, approvals, notes and review history for audit-readiness.

How AI Governance Hub connects framework requirements to Jira workflows

  • Register AI use cases, owners, vendors and business context.
  • Assess risk across privacy, security, customer impact, automation and regulated-domain factors.
  • Route reviews to security, privacy, responsible AI, legal and business stakeholders.
  • Maintain approval history, evidence references, audit notes and governance decisions.
  • Provide executive-ready summaries for AI governance, compliance and risk stakeholders.
