# User Guide — v18.0 Commercial Launch

**Product:** AI Governance Hub — One AI Governance Starter Report  
**Price:** ₹199 INR via Razorpay (display prices in other currencies are reference only)

## Getting a Starter Report

1. Go to [Pricing](https://aigovernancehub.ai/pricing.html)
2. **Upload** your CSV, TSV, TXT, or Excel export (max 5 MB)
3. Review the **free preview** (record counts, AI candidates, risk summary)
4. Enter your name and email, then **pay ₹199** via Razorpay
5. After verification, **download** your full HTML report
6. A confirmation email is sent (if email is configured)

## Accessing reports later

### Option A — My Reports (recommended)

1. Go to [Sign in](https://aigovernancehub.ai/login.html)
2. Enter your email — we send a **magic link** (no password)
3. Click the link within 15 minutes
4. View all purchased reports on **My Reports**
5. Download reports or invoices anytime during the retention period

### Option B — Recover without signing in

1. Go to [Recover My Report](https://aigovernancehub.ai/recover-report.html)
2. Enter the email used at checkout
3. Download reports from the list

## What you receive

- Governance score and rating
- AI candidate inventory
- Risk breakdown (high / medium / low)
- Framework context (EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF)
- Executive summary
- **Disclaimer:** Not legal certification or compliance approval

## Payment security

- Payments processed by **Razorpay**
- AI Governance Hub does **not** store card or UPI details
- Payment is verified **server-side** before report unlock

## Invoices

From My Reports, click **Download Invoice** for a simple HTML receipt after verified payment.
