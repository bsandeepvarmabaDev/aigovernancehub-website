User Guide

AI Governance Hub is organized around practical governance workflows inside Jira Cloud.

Dashboard

View AI governance metrics, risk distribution, review status, compliance readiness, and executive indicators.

Reviews

Manage AI review items, track approval status, assign reviewers, add comments, and create risk tickets for follow-up.

Models & Assets

Maintain visibility of AI use cases, models, systems, vendors, owners, and lifecycle status.

Compliance

Track readiness against governance frameworks and internal control expectations. The app supports governance workflow tracking and should not be treated as legal advice.

Vendors & Policies

Review AI vendors, third-party tools, policy references, governance obligations, and review evidence.

Operations

Monitor review operations, workflow health, SLA indicators, and action follow-ups.

Reports

Generate or export governance summaries for leadership, audit readiness, and operational review.

Notifications

Review alerts, workflow updates, and action reminders related to governance activity.
