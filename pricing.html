FAQ

Common questions

Is Starter different from Marketplace?

Yes. Starter is a website-only evaluation offer. Atlassian Marketplace is recommended for production team usage with Jira-native install and Atlassian billing.

What happens after I pay ₹499?

You receive a Razorpay payment confirmation by email. Share your Jira Cloud site URL with support@aigovernancehub.ai and our team will send Starter onboarding instructions.

Do I need Jira Cloud?

Yes. AI Governance Hub is built for Jira Cloud. Starter and Marketplace plans both require a Jira Cloud site.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Starter includes an upgrade path to Atlassian Marketplace when you are ready for production rollout.

Is payment secure?

Payments are processed by Razorpay. AI Governance Hub does not handle or store card details on this website.

Do you store card details?

No. Card details are handled by Razorpay. AI Governance Hub does not store payment card information.

Is this legal/compliance certification?

No. Framework mapping supports governance evidence and readiness workflows but does not certify legal compliance.

How do I contact support?

Support: support@aigovernancehub.ai. Sales: sales@aigovernancehub.ai.
