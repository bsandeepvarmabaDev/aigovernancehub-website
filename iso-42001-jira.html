ISO 42001 AI Management System Workflows in Jira Cloud

Updated: June 2026

AI Governance Hub helps teams support AI management system practices by structuring AI inventory, governance reviews, control evidence, and continuous improvement activity in Jira Cloud.

1. Why ISO 42001 matters

ISO 42001 provides a management-system approach for organizations that develop, provide, or use AI systems. It encourages repeatable governance practices, accountability, risk management, documentation, and continuous improvement.

2. How AI Governance Hub supports readiness

AI Governance Hub does not replace certification activity, but it can help teams organize the operational evidence and governance workflows needed to support an AI management system.

  • Maintain AI system and vendor inventory.
  • Document ownership, business purpose, and lifecycle status.
  • Route AI governance reviews and approvals.
  • Track risks, actions, and control evidence.
  • Maintain audit-ready review history and decision records.

3. Suggested ISO 42001 workflow

  • Register AI assets and owners.
  • Document intended purpose and business context.
  • Assess risks and required controls.
  • Assign review and approval responsibilities.
  • Capture evidence and decisions.
  • Review actions for continuous improvement.

4. Best fit teams

  • AI management system owners
  • Compliance teams
  • Internal audit teams
  • Security and privacy teams
  • AI product delivery teams
