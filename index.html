Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Jira to use AI Governance Hub?

The Jira Cloud app is the first deployment channel. The website assessment can be used for high-level evaluation while broader platform options are planned.

Is assessment data stored?

The current website assessment runs in the browser. The public site does not use a backend database for storing assessment submissions.

What do paid reports include?

Paid reviews can include governance findings, risk breakdown, recommended reviews, compliance mapping, vendor risk and audit readiness guidance.

Is Razorpay integrated?

Online checkout will be enabled after payment links are finalized. Until then, commercial requests go to sales.

Which frameworks are supported?

The platform vision includes EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 and PCI DSS mapping.

Is this legal or compliance advice?

No. AI Governance Hub provides workflow support and informational outputs. Final legal, audit and compliance decisions should be reviewed by qualified professionals.
